Tax Calculator

Is a utility that computes the Total Salary of an employee based on the employee's income for the year.

Brief description of each input box:
  1. Current Monthly Salary - Is the employee's latest salary per month.

  2. Pay Frequency - Is the frequency in which the employee receives his/her salary.

    • Monthly Paid employees have a total of 12 payout per year.
    • Semi - Monthly Paid employees have a total of 12 payout per year.

  3. Tax Status - Is the employee's current tax status:

    • Single or Married
    • Single with 1 dependent / Married with 1 dependent
    • Single with 2 dependent / Married with 2 dependent
    • Single with 3 dependent / Married with 3 dependent
    • Single with 4 dependent / Married with 4 dependent

  4. Remaining Payout - Is the number of remaining payout for the year.

    Monthly Paid employees have a total of 12 payout per year.

    Semi - Monthly Paid employees have a total of 12 payout per year.

    i.e. Monthly Paid employee with current payout on March. Remaining payout would be 9.

  5. Year to Date Income - Is the employee's total accumulated basic pay for the year

    i.e. Salary per payout, undertime, absences...

  6. Year to Date Bonus Income - Is the employee's total accumulated additional income and bonus income for the year i.e. Overtime Pay, Night Shift Differential Pay, 13month Pay, meal allowance, transpo allowance...
  7. Year to Date Tax Deductible - Is the employee's total tax excluded income for the year i.e. SSS, HDMF, PHIC.
  8. Year to Date Witholding Tax - Is the employee's total paid witholding tax for the year.
  9. Current Income - Is the employee's income for the current payout i.e. Salary per payout, undertime, absences.
  10. Current Additional Income - Is the employee's total accumulated additional income for the current payout i.e. Overtime Pay, Night Shift Differential Pay.
  11. Current Bonus - Is the employee's total accumulated bonus income for the current payout i.e. 13month Pay, meal allowance, transpo allowance.
  12. Current Tax Deductible - Is the employee's total tax excluded income for the current payout i.e. SSS, HDMF, PHIC.
